About Our Shop

Welcome to Ty's Barber Shop, where style meets precision. Located in the heart of Langebaan, we've been providing exceptional grooming services to our community since 2015.

Our skilled barbers are trained in the latest techniques to deliver the perfect cut and style for every client. We take pride in creating a welcoming atmosphere where you can relax and enjoy a premium grooming experience.
